Accounting Staff

You will be responsible for checking and verifying the company's financial transactions, carrying out recording and documentation, and being tasked with compiling accurate financial reports. In this role, we also expect you to understand a general overview of taxes, particularly those related to financial transactions.

Make Office Financial Bookkeeping

The job of an accounting staff is to keep office financial books. To make your own bookkeeping, there are various formats, but usually companies have prepared standard bookkeeping accounting reports complete with the company's needs.

Making financial reporting

his is the main task of accounting staff. This financial report contains the company's financial recapitulation data which has been signed and will be submitted directly to the management. Usually the leadership does not carry out checks directly but through the financial reports that you make.

Posting Operational Journals

The next accounting task is to post journal entries. Journal posting is recording all expenditure and income activities from the company's operational activities, after which entering them into the company's accounting ledger. The ledger itself is a collection of data and a recapitulation of accounting reports owned by the company.

Preparing Reports Regarding Company Taxation

Accounting staff is the person responsible for making reports regarding company taxation. Making or compiling reports regarding taxation includes calculating all taxes, for example income tax imposed on each employee.
